# Relationship Between Research Questions and Survey Questions

| RQ | Analytical focus | Open-ended question applied to students |
|----|------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **RQ1** | AI integration in modern architectures | How did you perceive the integration of Artificial Intelligence in the studied architectures, such as Microservices, Event-Driven, Serverless, or BFF? What opportunities or challenges would you highlight? |
| **RQ2** | Architectural modeling and communication | How did the use of models and approaches, such as arc42, C4 Model, and UML, help to understand and communicate architectural decisions in solutions involving AI and cloud computing? |
| **RQ3** | Learning and practical application | What contributed most to your learning about modern solution architecture using AI, and how do you intend to apply this knowledge in future projects? |
