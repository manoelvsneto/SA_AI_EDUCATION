# Indications of Curricular Gaps

**Inferred from RQ3 analysis**

| Gap indication | Analytical interpretation |
|----------------|--------------------------|
| **Low mention of MLOps** | Students rarely related AI to the operational lifecycle of models in production. |
| **Low mention of *model drift*** | Model evolution and degradation over time did not appear as a recurrent concern. |
| **Low mention of AI governance** | Aspects such as auditing, accountability, compliance, and model control were poorly evidenced. |
| **Low mention of continuous observability** | Monitoring, metrics, logs, and model traceability did not appear expressively. |
| **Low mention of explainability** | The ability to interpret model decisions was not highlighted as a frequent architectural concern. |
